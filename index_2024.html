Jingbo Zhang

Email  /  CV  /  Bio  /  Google Scholar  /  Twitter  /  Github

I am a Ph.D. Candidate supervised by Prof. Jing Liao at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. I have broad research interests in machine learning, pattern recognition, computer vision, and graphics. Recently, I am focusing on 3D/4D reconstruction and generation, 3D scene editing, 2D/3D inpainting, depth estimation, neural rendering, and diffusion models.

If you have any questions to discuss or are interested in collaboration, please feel free to contact me.
